## 🔍 Job Risk Analysis Breakdown

### 1. Red Flags Identified 🚩
<!-- Claude ne jo negative points ya risks dikhaye unhe yahan likhein -->
* **Vague Job Description:** Roles and responsibilities are overly broad (e.g., handling both complete frontend and backend single-handedly).
* **Unrealistic Expectations:** Requirement of 4+ years of experience in a recently launched framework or tool.
* **Work-Life Balance:** Job description explicitly mentions "willingness to work on weekends" or "high-pressure environment".

### 2. Positive Signals 👍
<!-- Jo acche points ya benefits report me aaye unhe yahan dalein -->
* **Clear Tech Stack:** Explicitly mentions modern tech stacks like Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Node.js, and clean architecture patterns.
* **Structured Growth:** Transparent mention of mentorship programs and clear performance review cycles.
* **Company Reputation:** Established presence in the industry with positive employee feedback on external portals.

## 💡 Generated Interview Questions to Ask the Recruiter
Claude dwara generate kiye gaye targeted questions jo aap interview me pouch sakte hain:
